Getting Started

Creating Your First Plan

  1. Open PlanAwAi and tap "Create a plan" on the home screen
  2. Enter your location - This can be your current location or anywhere you're planning to visit
  3. Select your date - Choose when you want to do these activities
  4. Describe your vibe - Tell us what you're in the mood for (e.g., "romantic dinner", "outdoor adventure", "cultural exploration")
  5. Browse AI suggestions - Review the curated list of activities that match your preferences
  6. Select activities - Choose the ones that interest you most
  7. Review your plan - See your optimized itinerary with timing and details
  8. Save and enjoy - Your plan is ready to execute!

Troubleshooting

The app isn't finding activities in my location

Try these steps:

  • Make sure location services are enabled for PlanAwAi in your device settings
  • Check that you have a stable internet connection
  • Try entering a more specific location (e.g., "Downtown Seattle" instead of just "Seattle")
  • If you're in a remote area, try searching for activities in the nearest major city
The AI suggestions don't match what I'm looking for

Help us understand you better:

  • Be more specific in your description (e.g., "quiet coffee shops with WiFi" instead of "coffee")
  • Use the activity swapping feature to find better matches
  • Mark activities as favorites to help our AI learn your preferences
  • Try different keywords or phrases to describe what you're looking for
The app is running slowly or crashing

Try these solutions:

  • Close and restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version of PlanAwAi installed
  • Check that your device has sufficient storage space
  • If problems persist, contact our support team

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PlanAwAi free to use?

PlanAwAi offers both free and premium features. You can create and save plans for free, with premium features available for enhanced personalization and additional planning tools.

Can I use PlanAwAi offline?

While you need an internet connection to discover new activities and create plans, you can view your saved plans offline once they're created.

How does the AI learn my preferences?

Our AI learns from your activity selections, favorites, and the types of plans you create and complete. The more you use PlanAwAi, the better it becomes at suggesting activities you'll love.

Can I share my plans with friends?

Yes! You can easily share your plans with friends and family through various sharing options available in the app.

What devices are supported?

PlanAwAi is available for iOS devices running iOS 13.0 or later, including iPhone and iPad.
